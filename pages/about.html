Company Overview

Who We Are

Blue Horizon Automation Research (BHAR) is an Indian technology company specializing in Robotic Process Automation (RPA), custom web application development, and business process consulting. Founded with a mission to make intelligent automation accessible to businesses of all sizes across India.

We don't just deliver solutions — we build future-ready systems tailored to your unique business context. From automating repetitive workflows to building full-scale digital platforms, we collaborate closely with clients at every step.

Our team of engineers, designers, and consultants brings deep expertise in RPA (UiPath), full-stack web development, Flutter mobile apps, and business strategy. We've partnered with IIT Bombay and multiple enterprises across India, delivering measurable outcomes.
