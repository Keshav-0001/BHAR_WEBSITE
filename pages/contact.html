Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions we receive before starting a project.

What industries do you work with?

We serve finance, insurance, real estate, education, manufacturing, retail, and government — any industry with repetitive processes to automate.

How long does an RPA project take?

Typical RPA implementations range from 4–12 weeks depending on scope. A simple bot can be live in 2 weeks; enterprise rollouts take longer.

Do you offer a free consultation?

Yes! We offer a free 30-minute discovery call where we assess your processes and identify automation opportunities — no obligation.

What is the minimum project size?

We work with businesses of all sizes — from SMEs to large enterprises. Every project is quoted based on scope, not company size.

Do you provide post-deployment support?

Absolutely. All projects include a post-go-live support period, and we offer ongoing maintenance packages tailored to your needs.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Yes. Our RPA bots and web apps integrate seamlessly with SAP, Tally, Oracle, Salesforce, and most ERP/CRM systems through APIs.
